Popular Spells

Expecto Patronum

The Patronus Charm

Conjures a spirit guardian that protects against Dementors and can send messages.

Expelliarmus

The Disarming Charm

Forces the victim to release whatever they're holding. Harry's signature spell.

Wingardium Leviosa

The Levitation Charm

Makes objects fly or levitate. The first spell taught at Hogwarts.

Accio

The Summoning Charm

Summons an object toward the caster, potentially over great distances.
